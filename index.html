About BetPro

Kenya's tailoring house for everyday and special-occasion wear.

BetPro was founded on a simple idea: tailoring should be reliable, affordable, and genuinely professional — the kind of service that respects your time and your garments equally.

We provide high-quality tailoring, clothing repairs, custom designs, alterations, and fashion solutions with professionalism, affordability, and fast delivery across Kenya. From a torn school uniform to a made-to-measure suit, our tailors bring the same care to every order.
